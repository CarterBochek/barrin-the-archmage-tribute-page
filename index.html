Barrin, the Tolarian Archmage

"There is no age at which you stop learning."

  • It is said that Barrin's full name is Barrinalo. When writing his name, the archmage ran out of ink and his name stopped short at Barrin, which he decided was a great nickname.
  • Archmage Barrin was born in the Kjeldor Kingdom of Dominaria during the iceage.
  • At birth it was prophesized that Barrin would become the most powerful magician of all Dominaria.
  • In the Magic the Gathering Trading card game, Barrin costs 3 mana, 2 Blue and 1 of any color.
  • It is hypothesized that Barrin's last spell cast was obliterate. A very manevolent spell filled with destruction and despair in its' wake.
